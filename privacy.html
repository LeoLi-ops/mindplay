MindPlay Privacy Policy

MindPlay ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

MindPlay itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or personal profile.

All gameplay progress and game-related data are stored locally on your device.

2. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, such as device identifiers, approximate location, and usage data in order to provide and improve advertising services.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Data Sharing

The App itself does not sell or share personal information with third parties. However, third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob may process limited technical data in order to display advertisements.

4. Third-Party Services

MindPlay may use the following third-party services:

• Google AdMob (Advertising)
• Firebase services (such as analytics or crash reporting)

These services may collect limited diagnostic or device information to maintain and improve the App.

5. Data Security

Most gameplay data is stored locally on your device. We take reasonable measures to protect the security of the App and its services.

6. Children's Privacy

MindPlay does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will address the issue.

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App's features or data practices change. Any updates will be published on this page.
