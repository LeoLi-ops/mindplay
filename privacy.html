MindPlay Privacy Policy

MindPlay ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, track, or share any personal information.

The App does not collect:

• Device identifiers
• Advertising IDs
• IP addresses
• Location data
• Usage analytics
• Personal profiles

All game data is stored locally on your device only.

2. Use of Information

Since no personal data is collected, no user information is used, analyzed, or processed on external servers.

All gameplay data remains on your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers.

4. Third-Party Services

MindPlay does not use third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

No external services receive your personal information.

5. Data Security

All game-related data is stored locally on your device.

We do not transmit any personal data over the internet.

6. Children's Privacy

MindPlay does not collect any personal information from users of any age.

No age-based restrictions are applied.

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App's data practices change.

Any updates will be published within the App.
